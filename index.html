Problem

Approximately 7 in 10 people take at least one prescription medication.
For a patient, the most important task is to take thier medication
Many people forget to refill thier medication in a timely manner.
This leads to complications in a patient's treatment. Forgetting
to take one's medication can have deadly results, as you can read here.

Medications Are Important

Medications are meant to be taken as prescribed in order for a patient's health to imporve. For this to happen, a patient must always have pills available to them.

Pills Run Out

Pills are bound to run out, which can lead to people not refilling thier prescriptions. This is something that is dangerous for a patient whose life relies on pills.

Hard For People To Refill

Not everyone can go refill thier medications, especially the ones who take the most medications, people of age and people who are extremely sick.

Solution

A pill bottle that automaticly orders a new
bottle of pills when someone is running low.
This would eliminate the chance of a person
being short of medication.

Parts

Creating this product would be very simple. It would only need four things in addition to pill bottle and a small battery.

  • Weight Sensor

    To Calculate Mass of Pills

  • NFC Tag

    To Store Information of pills

  • An App

    To display information of pills

  • Bluetooth Sensor

    To send information to the app

Impact

The impact of this IOT device would be seen mostly on people suffering
from chronic illnesse and people of age. They would always have access
to their medications as they would never run out of thier pills. This makes
their lives easier, increasing the quality of life.

Allways Have Sufficient Pills

When a low ammount of pills is detected, a new bottle will be dilivered to your house.

Never Run Out

As a new bottle will be dilivered, you will not have to worry about running out of pills

No Need To Refill

People who need pills the most would not have to rely on others to get their pills.
